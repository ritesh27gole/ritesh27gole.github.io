Self Balancing Delivery Bike

  • Designed a self-balancing flywheel based delivery bike with LQR based controller for balancing
  • Developed algorithms for driving, steering, road-following and color detection and simulated it in CoppeliaSim
  • Mathematically modeled it using the Euler-lagrangian method, calculated its controllability, observability and stability, and used pole placement method to get required controller parameters
